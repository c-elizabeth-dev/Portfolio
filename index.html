About me

My journey to a fulfilling career in software development has been marked by determination, a can-do attitude, and a desire to learn. I embarked on a transformative 12-week intensive bootcamp at Just IT Training Ltd, London, where I delved into both front-end and back-end development.
This educational journey not only exposed me to essential programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL but also equipped me with additional problem-solving skills, creativity, and confidence.
Throughout my career, including my time in retail and customer service, I've excelled in building strong rapport with clients and other business owners.
My persistence, tenacity, and amiable disposition have been essential in developing and maintaining lasting relationships. When faced with challenges, I approach them with a goal to find solutions and move forward. This adaptability and quick-thinking nature have been critical in my career journey.
As I work towards a future as a junior software developer, I intend to leverage my creative eye, proficient graphic design skills, and strong knowledge of Photoshop, Procreate, and other design tools, alongside my more analytical side. My ability to connect with people, solve problems efficiently, and embrace new learning challenges positions me to thrive in this exciting transition, where I am enthusiastic about the opportunities and experiences that lie ahead.
